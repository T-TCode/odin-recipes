Macaroni Salad Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for macaroni salad

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Mayo
  • Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Seasonings
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Bell pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the macaroni in salted water
  2. Mix the mayo, sugar, vinegar, seasonings, celery, onion, and bell peppers together
  3. Stir in macaroni into the mixture and let it chill in the refrigerator

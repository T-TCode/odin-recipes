Hamburger Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for a hamburger

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2lb lean ground beef
  • Chopped onions
  • Sliced cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Combine the all ingredients in a bowl
  2. Make patties
  3. Grill the patties until the center is no longer pink

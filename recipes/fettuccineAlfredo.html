Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe

Home

Description

This is a recipe for a classic fettuccine alfredo

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • Romano and parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

    Cook the pasta according to the directions on the box
    Melt the butter and cream together on the stovetop. Add in salt, pepper, and your cheeses
    Add the pasta to the pan and mix well to coat the pasta in sauce
